Privacy Policy

HOA Contracts values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, HOA or association information, and any details you submit through our forms.

To improve our forms and website experience, we may also collect information you enter into our online forms even if you do not complete or submit the form. This helps us understand where forms can be made easier to use. Information collected from incomplete forms is used for analytics and service-improvement purposes and is not used to contact you unless you submit the form and provide your consent.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through this website may be used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and review requests
  • Connect you with insurance professionals or service providers
  • Evaluate HOA insurance and vendor contract opportunities
  • Improve our website and services
  • Communicate with you regarding requested services

Information Sharing

HOA Contracts may share submitted information with licensed insurance agencies, service providers, contractors, or vendor partners relevant to your inquiry or requested review services.

We do not sell personal information to unrelated third parties for general marketing purposes.

Communication Consent

By submitting information through this website, you consent to be contacted by phone, text message, and email regarding HOA insurance, vendor review services, and related opportunities, including through automated technology where permitted by law.

Cookies & Analytics

This website may use cookies, analytics tools, and advertising technologies including Google Analytics and Google Ads to better understand website traffic and improve marketing performance.

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites or providers. HOA Contracts is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect submitted information, but no internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Policy Updates

HOA Contracts may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the HOA Contracts website.
