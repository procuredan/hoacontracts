HOA Insurance Carrier Review: Comparing Markets, Pricing & Renewal Strategy

Not every carrier underwrites HOAs the same way. Appetite, pricing, and coverage structure vary widely, and a policy that renews on autopilot is rarely the most competitive option available to your community.

Why carrier appetite matters

Some carriers specialize in condo and HOA risk; others price it conservatively because it isn't their focus. Matching your association to a carrier that wants the business usually means better pricing and better coverage terms.

What we benchmark

We compare your current program against available markets on limits, deductible structure, replacement cost, D&O, and total premium — so your board can see how your renewal stacks up before signing.

Building a renewal strategy

A good review isn't just this year's price. It's a strategy: how to position your community, what to fix before the next renewal, and how to avoid year-over-year premium creep.
