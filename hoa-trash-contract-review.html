Where Associations Often Overpay

Many HOAs continue paying for oversized service plans, outdated pricing structures, or inefficient pickup schedules that no longer fit the property’s needs.

  • Too many scheduled pickups
  • Outdated service agreements
  • Annual pricing increases
  • Unused or oversized containers
  • Limited vendor comparison

Reduce Waste Service Costs

Even small adjustments to trash service agreements can create meaningful annual savings for an association. A proper review can help determine whether better pricing or service options may exist.
