Common Questions

Board Liability FAQ

What is D&O coverage for an HOA?

Directors & officers coverage helps protect board members from personal liability for decisions and actions taken in their role for the association.

Why does it matter?

Board members are volunteers, but lawsuits over decisions, elections, or enforcement can name them personally. Adequate D&O limits and defense coverage matter.

Is the review free?

Yes — free, no obligation, and your information is never sold or shared.
