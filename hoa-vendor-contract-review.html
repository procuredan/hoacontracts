Many HOA and condo associations work with the same vendors for years without reviewing whether pricing, service levels, or contract terms are still competitive.

What We Evaluate

  • Vendor pricing and recurring increases
  • Service agreement terms
  • Automatic renewals and cancellation clauses
  • Vendor performance expectations
  • Competitive market comparisons
  • Potential savings opportunities

Why HOA Vendor Reviews Matter

Over time, vendor contracts can drift away from current market pricing. Without regular review, associations may unknowingly overpay or accept declining service quality.

A proper contract review helps HOA boards better understand where opportunities may exist to reduce costs, improve vendor accountability, and protect the association’s budget.
