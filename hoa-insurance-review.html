Why Many HOA Associations Overpay For Insurance

Many HOA boards simply renew existing policies without shopping the market or reviewing whether the association’s needs have changed. Over time, this can lead to:

  • Higher premiums than necessary
  • Outdated or duplicated coverage
  • Insufficient deductibles or reserve planning
  • Missed opportunities to improve coverage terms
