What Is Included In An HOA Internet & Cable Review?

We help associations look at the key areas that often affect cost, service quality, and contract value.

  • Bulk internet or cable agreement pricing

  • Renewal dates and automatic renewal clauses

Why HOA Cable & Internet Agreements Are Often Overlooked

Many associations focus on visible expenses like landscaping or trash service, while cable and internet agreements quietly renew in the background. These agreements can become outdated as technology, resident expectations, and provider pricing change.

A review can help determine whether the association has better options available, whether the current agreement is still competitive, and whether there are missed revenue or service opportunities.

Common HOA Internet & Cable Contract Issues

  • Contracts that automatically renew without board review
  • Pricing that no longer matches current market options
  • Limited provider flexibility
  • Resident complaints about speed, reliability, or service
  • Missed revenue opportunities from access or bulk agreements
  • Unclear responsibility for equipment, wiring, or common-area access
