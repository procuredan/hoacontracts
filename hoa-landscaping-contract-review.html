HOA Landscaping Contract Review

Landscaping is one of the largest recurring expenses for many HOA and condo associations. Over time, contracts often renew automatically while pricing increases and service quality changes.

Our HOA landscaping contract review helps associations evaluate pricing, service levels, vendor performance, and contract terms to identify potential savings and better options.

  • Scope of work and maintenance frequency
  • Vendor performance standards
  • Irrigation and water management concerns
  • Renewal terms and cancellation clauses
  • Competitive bidding opportunities

Common HOA Landscaping Contract Problems

Many associations continue paying higher landscaping costs simply because contracts are not regularly reviewed or competitively bid.

  • Automatic renewals with annual price increases
  • Reduced service quality over time
  • Unclear maintenance expectations
  • Overlapping or unnecessary services
  • Poor communication or accountability
